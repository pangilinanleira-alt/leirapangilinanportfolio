S.Y. 25-26











































  • The Miting de Advance was an important event during the school year in deciding our SSLG leaders. It teaches students to listen and vote wisely for upcoming leaders, especially in the future as the hope of our country.

  • The Booklandia event during English Month serves as a reminder of the power of reading. Bringing these characters to life, we learn about their values and morals that we have also learned throughout life.

  • Math Month gave us a chance to show off our talents not only in numbers and puzzles, but also in the arts and singing. It was a month full of fun and adventure as a student interested in the field of mathematics.

  • The Noli Me Tangere we presented, while I played Donya Victorina, gave us a chance to reflect on our country's culture and literature during the time of the Spanish colonization. It also gave us time to bond with one another and grow closer as a section.
